Supplements


Supplements aren’t entirely necessary, but can be very convenient and useful if you are having trouble reaching your nutrient goals. Some, like protein powder, are great to round off your diet, and others, like steroids, are likely unnecssary and potentially harmful.

Let’s go over some popular recommended supplements

Protein Powder


Protein powder is probably the most popular supplement and for good reason.

It’s very convenient to get 20-30g of protein in a shake and with a low calorie count.

While protein powder isn’t 100% necessary, it can be a great tool to reach your protein goals if you’e finding it difficult to eat enough!

Vitamins


The term “vitamins” covers a lot, including amino acids, omega-3 pills, B3 pills, and multivitamins.

My take on vitamins is to just take it if you think you need it. In general, I feel that multivitamins are a pretty safe bet.

However, things like amino acids and specific nutrients like Vitamin B3, I would make sure that you need it before you take it.
